About Me

I decided to look for a job in the tech field because programing and coding is something i have always loved doing and its also a job that can often be worked remotely, which will allow me to continue to tour in my music career but also work in the tech field which i am super excited to enter. the only thing better than a dream job is to have two dream jobs.
